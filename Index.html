Adam Karwoski

Game systems designer and engineer

My name is Adam Karwoski and I am a gameplay programmer for video games. I started playing video games at a young age and it quickly became my favorite hobby. I decided early on I wanted to bring the joy I got from games to others and that brought me to where I am today. I am team focused and open to learning new skills to help create fun experiences for people to enjoy, along with being adaptable. I graduated from Savannah College of Art and Design with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Interactive Design and Game Development.
