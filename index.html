Game systems designer and engineer

My name is Adam Karwoski and I am a gameplay programmer for video games. Video games were a large part of my childhood and I decided to bring the joys that video games brought me to others. My strengths include being team focused, adaptable, and open to learning new skills to help create fun experiences for people to enjoy. I graduated from Savannah College of Art and Design with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Interactive Design and Game Development.
