About Me

I decided early on I wanted to bring the joy I got from games to others and that brought me to where I am today. I am team focused and open to learning new skills to help create fun experiences for people to enjoy, along with being adaptable. I graduated from Savannah College of Art and Design with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Interactive Design and Game Development.
